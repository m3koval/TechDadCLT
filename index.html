Also for adult children caring for aging parents

Protecting the parents
who raised you.

Seniors lose more money to online scams than any other age group. Your mom or dad has a smartphone — and it's almost certainly wide open. We can change that.

IRS & Medicare impersonation calls

Scammers posing as government agencies demand immediate payment or personal information by phone. One of the most financially devastating and common scams targeting seniors.

Tech support fraud

"Your computer has a virus" pop-ups and fake Microsoft/Apple calls. Scammers gain remote access, steal banking credentials, and charge hundreds for repairs that were never needed.

Phishing emails & fake links

Convincing emails from "Amazon," "your bank," or "FedEx" steal passwords and account info. Without the right protections, one click can hand over everything.

Social media & romance scams

Fake Facebook marketplace deals, lottery notifications, and "new friends" who eventually ask for money or gift cards. Seniors are targeted specifically because they tend to be more trusting.
